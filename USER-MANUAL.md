# Super Famille Movies — User Manual

**Your family's shared app for tracking movies and series, and finding something great to watch tonight.**

Open the app at: **https://family-movie-night-tau.vercel.app**

---

## Getting started

When you open the app, you'll be asked **"Who's watching?"** — just tap your avatar to identify yourself. The app remembers you on your device, so next time you only need to confirm with one tap.

**Blue dot on your avatar?** That means there are entries in the catalogue you haven't voted on yet (no interest record at all). Tap any avatar on a card to vote — even setting it to neutral ("I don't mind") counts as a vote and clears the dot for that entry.

To switch to someone else mid-session, tap the small avatar in the top-right corner of any screen. This opens a menu with quick links to the Catalogue, Movie Night, and Settings, as well as a "Change user" option.

---

## The home screen

This is your catalogue — every movie and series the family has added.

Each card shows:
- The **poster**, title, type (Movie / Series), length, platform and genre
- A row of **family avatars** grouped by interest: green ring = wants to watch, grey = neutral, red = doesn't want to watch. A small checkmark on an avatar means that person has already seen it.
- A speech bubble icon if someone has left a comment.

**Quick-toggle your interest** without leaving the home screen: tap your own avatar on any card to cycle through neutral → yes → no → neutral. A dimmed avatar means that person hasn't voted yet; a grey ring means they explicitly don't mind.

**Filter and sort the list** using the bar at the top:
- Filter by type (Movies / Series)
- Filter by platform
- Filter by genre
- Cycle the interest filter: **★ / ?** (inactive) → **★ Yes** (only your yes votes) → **? Unvoted** (entries you haven't voted on yet) → back to all
- Hide entries everyone has already watched
- Toggle sort between **Recent first** (default) and **A→Z** alphabetical

## Adding a movie or series

Tap the **+** button in the top-right corner.

1. **Search** — type a title. Results from TMDB appear with poster, year and type. Tap the right one.
2. **Review details** — the title, duration, genre, cast, release year, summary, trailer, and platform are filled in automatically. The platform is detected from streaming services available in France (e.g. Netflix, Disney+). Edit anything that looks wrong. **"Suggested by"** defaults to you — change it if someone else found it. Add any notes.
3. **Set interests** — tap each person's avatar to record their interest right now. You can do this on everyone's behalf in one go — handy when you're all in the same room.
4. Tap **Save** — it appears in the catalogue immediately on everyone's device.

If a title isn't on TMDB, tap **"Enter manually"** to fill in everything yourself.

## The detail page

Tap anywhere on a card (not the avatar) to open the full detail page.

Here you can:
- Read the full **summary**, with release year and main cast shown below the title
- Watch the **trailer** (tap the "Watch trailer" button — opens YouTube)
- See and update **everyone's interest** — tap an avatar to cycle their state
- Toggle **"Mark watched"** for each person
- Read and write **comments** — tap your own comment area (or "Add a comment…") to type something. Other people's comments are read-only.
- For series: track **episode progress** (see below)

---

## Tracking series progress

On a series detail page, if anyone has started watching, a **Series progress** section appears.

- Each family member shows their current position, e.g. **S2 E4**
- Tap the **+** button to log the next episode — the season advances automatically at the end of each season
- Tap the **S2 E4** label to set your position manually (useful when catching up)
- You can see at a glance who is ahead and who is behind
- When you reach the last episode, your row automatically shows **Finished ✓** and you're marked as watched

## Movie Night mode

Tap the **🎬 Movie Night** button on the home screen when you're ready to pick something to watch.

**Step 1 — Setup:**
- Tick who is present tonight
- Enter how much time you have (e.g. `2h`, `90min`, `1h30`)
- Tap **Find something to watch**

**Step 2 — Results:**

| List | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Perfect matches** | Everyone present is willing to watch, nobody has seen it, and it fits in your time |
| **Good, but…** | Worth considering — each card shows exactly why it's not a perfect match (someone is away and wants to see it, someone has already seen it, or it runs a bit long) |
| **Could watch over 2 evenings** | Only shown when nothing else fits — too long for tonight but no other issues |
| **Not everyone is on board** | Last resort — shows who said no, so you can have a conversation |

Tap any result card to go to its detail page.

---

## Removing an entry

On any detail page, tap **Remove** (top-right, next to Edit). A confirmation prompt appears — tap **Remove** again to delete it permanently. This removes all interests, comments and progress for that entry.

---

## Tips

- **One device, whole family**: you can pass the phone around the table. Tap the avatar in the corner to switch user before making changes.
- **Real-time updates**: if two people are using the app at the same time, changes (interests, comments, progress) appear on both screens automatically — no refresh needed.
- **Dark mode**: the app automatically switches to a dark theme when your phone is in night mode.
- **Install on your phone**: on iPhone, open in Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen". On Android, open in Chrome → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app". It works like a regular app from your home screen.
